About

B Charles is a multi-faceted theatre technician based in Chennai, India, who is equally adept at lighting design, sound, stage and event management.

Groomed by the late theatre veteran Mithran Devanesan, Charles’s experience in theatre spans more than 12 years working in multiple backstage departments like stage and set design, lighting design and sound.

He has also attended special workshops to get advanced training in lighting design and technology by Dee Ashworth (UK) and Jonathan O’ Hear (Switzerland) organized by Bangalore- based Attakkalari.

Charles holds a Bachelor’s Degree in History and an MSC in Psychology from The Madras University. He has an avid interest in world cinema and a keen eye for script, cinematography and set design.
